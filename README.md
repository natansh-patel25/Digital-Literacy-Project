## PROJECT OVERVIEW

This project is developed as part of the Digital Literacy course. The objective of this project is to build essential digital skills such as using online tools, creating a professional digital presence, understanding email etiquette, and being aware of cybercrime and online safety. Through this project, I explored various digital platforms and learned how to use them effectively for academic and professional purposes.

---

## TASK 1: DIGITAL LITERACY INFOGRAPHIC

In this task, I created a digital infographic using Canva. The infographic explains the concept of digital literacy, highlights useful digital tools for students, and includes safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette. The aim was to present important information in a simple and visually appealing format.

---

## TASK 2: DIGITAL PORTFOLIO

In this task, I created and updated my profiles on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. These platforms help in building a professional identity and showcasing skills. Setting up these profiles helped me understand the importance of maintaining a digital presence for future career opportunities.

---

## TASK 3: CODING AND COLLABORATION PLATFORMS

In this task, I explored coding and collaboration tools. I solved a beginner-level problem on HackerRank to understand basic problem-solving. I also created a Google Form titled "Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz" and connected it with Google Sheets to collect responses.

## TASK 4: EMAIL ETIQUETTE

In this task, I drafted two professional emails. One email was written to request an assignment extension, and the other was written to apply for a summer internship opportunity. I also created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts to promote responsible online behavior.

---

## TASK 5: CYBERCRIME AWARENESS

In this task, I created a case study on UPI payment fraud, explaining how such scams occur, who is targeted, and their consequences. I also prepared a prevention checklist to help users stay safe online and avoid cybercrime.

This project helped me understand the importance of digital literacy in today’s world.
