\## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist



\- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.

\- Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps, especially if you are not expecting any payment.

\- Always double-check the sender’s identity before responding to calls, emails, or messages related to money.

\- Avoid clicking on suspicious links received through SMS, WhatsApp, or email, as they may lead to phishing websites.

\- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts and change them periodically.

\- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts such as email, banking apps, and social media.

\- Install applications only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store.

\- Regularly monitor your bank transactions and report any unusual activity immediately.

\- Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions or logging into sensitive accounts.

\- Log out from shared or public devices after using your accounts.
